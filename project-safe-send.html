Safe Send — Precision Transport Packaging

Precision component packaging that protects high-value assemblies from shock, vibration, heat, and humidity while logging the trip.

Overview My Role Final Design Review

Overview

Two-box isolation system: rigid outer frame and a suspended inner payload box on springs/wire-rope isolators. Onboard sensors captures acceleration, temperature, and humidity to an SD card. My specific roles on the team focused on FEA and 3D Printing efforts.

My Role

Project Management

  • Organized and coordinated weekly meetings.
  • Developed meeting agendas and documented progress.
  • Spearheaded group efforts for design reviews and reports.

Finite Element Analysis

  • Developed a transient thermal response model in Ansys to predict heat exposure.
  • Shock modeling for spring sizing; test planning and data collection.
  • Vibration modal analysis to ensure damping effect.

3D Printing

  • Isolation system CAD & build.
  • 3D printed structure and clamping mechanism.
  • Utilized rubber gasket to provide protective seal.
