Safe Send — Precision Transport Packaging

Precision component packaging that protects high-value assemblies from shock, vibration, heat, and humidity while logging the trip.

Final Design Review (PDF) Overview Gallery

Overview

Two-box isolation system: rigid outer frame and a suspended inner payload box on springs/wire-rope isolators. Onboard logger captures acceleration, temperature, and humidity to an SD card. CAD-driven design for manufacturability and serviceability.

My Role & Tools

Responsibilities

  • Isolation system CAD & build; 3D-printed mounts and fixtures.
  • Shock modeling for spring sizing; test planning and data collection.
  • Logger wiring & workflow; report writing and reviews.
