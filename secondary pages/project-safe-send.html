Safe Send — Precision Transport Packaging

Clean-room compatible packaging that protects high-value assemblies from shock, vibration, heat, and humidity while logging the trip. Validated via drop, vibration, and thermal tests; designed for manufacturability and rental business model.

Download Final Design Review (PDF) See Gallery

Overview

We built a two-box isolation system: an outer framed shell with panels and a suspended inner payload box using springs and wire-rope isolators. An Arduino-based logger (dual ADXL373 accelerometers + DHT22 sensors) captures acceleration, temperature, and humidity to an SD card. Finite-element shock modeling informed spring choice and damping; design changes between CDR and FDR improved sealing and wiring. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Key Requirements

< 15 g inner box @ 1 m drop; < 0.5 °C/min at 45 °C gradient; vibration attenuation >3 dB ≥10 Hz; track shock/thermal/humidity. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Results

Shock

Inner box peak acceleration 13.1 g (spec met) vs outer 16.2 g in 1 m drop tests. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

Thermal

At 35 °C gradient, inner box rise ~0.033 °C/min; projected 0.042 °C/min at 45 °C (spec met). :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Vibration

Attenuation 5–15 dB beyond 20 Hz; PSD peak near 5 Hz; overall target achieved at higher bands. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
