Starship Delivery Robot — Wi-Fi RC Toy

Remote-controlled replica inspired by campus delivery robots. Custom chassis and linkages designed in PTC Creo; Arduino firmware drives DC and servo actuators.

System Design Gallery

System Design

Electronics & Control

  • Arduino reads RC/Wi-Fi commands and outputs PWM to motor driver and servos.
  • Designed a simple control interface allowing anyone to operate with ease.

Mechanical

  • 3D-printed mounts for electronics and frame.
  • Removable shell with interference fit for quick adjustments and changes.
  • Skeleton model for rocker bogey mechanism and two arm linkage for opening.
