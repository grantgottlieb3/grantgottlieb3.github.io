Starship Delivery Robot — Wi-Fi RC Toy

Remote-controlled replica inspired by campus delivery robots. Custom chassis and linkages designed in PTC Creo; Arduino firmware drives DC and servo actuators.

View Demo (add link) System Design Gallery

System Design

Electronics & Control

  • Arduino reads RC/Wi-Fi commands and outputs PWM to motor driver and servos.
  • Optional lighting and battery monitoring for safe cutoff.

Mechanical

  • PTC Creo CAD; 3D-printed mounts for electronics and steering linkages.
  • Serviceable shell with quick fasteners; labeled looms and strain relief.
