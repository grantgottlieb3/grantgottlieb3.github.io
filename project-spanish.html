Spanish Immersion — Conversational Web App

Clean, approachable practice for learners who can’t study abroad—scenes, roles, and goals that feel like real Madrid.

Overview Scenes Roadmap

Overview

The app organizes short, goal-driven scenes (airport, café, metro, housing) by difficulty and keeps you speaking in natural Spanish. You pick a scene, a role, and a CEFR level; the system guides you to a clear outcome in a few minutes.

Note: the AI engine is intentionally disabled here until I finalize the design.

Level

Your level adjusts tone, speed, and complexity across scenes.

Scenes

Arrival & Passport Control

Barajas airport essentials: passport, luggage, and getting into the city.

Café Like a Local

Order clearly, fix small mistakes politely, and sound natural.

New Flat, New Roommate

Introduce yourself and agree on three simple house norms.

Colloquialisms & Flamenco

Pick up Madrid expressions and plan a tablao visit.

Meet & Make Plans

Set a concrete day, time, and place with someone new.

First Day of Class

Introduce yourself and understand the syllabus and resources.
