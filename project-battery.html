Model & Pack

Results

Cycle 1 — 0.2C/0.2C

Efficiency ≈ 96.7%; 58.0 kWh in, 56.1 kWh out; healthy SOC profile. :contentReference[oaicite:23]{index=23}

Cycle 2 — 2C/0.2C

Efficiency ≈ 84.0%; losses increase with fast charge current; charge ~0.4 h. :contentReference[oaicite:24]{index=24}

Cycle 3 — 2C/2C

Efficiency ≈ 72.3%; short, high-current cycle amplifies internal loss. :contentReference[oaicite:25]{index=25}

US06 Drive Cycle (Bonus)

Delivered ~51.5 kWh over ~162 miles (~3.4 h). Recharge energy: 58.0 kWh @0.2C vs 66.8 kWh @2C, confirming higher fast-charge losses. :contentReference[oaicite:26]{index=26}
