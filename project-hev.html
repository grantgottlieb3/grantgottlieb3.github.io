1) Parallel HEV — Drive-Cycle Simulation

MATLAB/Simulink model of a parallel HEV with supervisory control to maintain SOC near 0.5, and CVT logic. Evaluated UDDS (city), HWFET (highway), and US06 (aggressive) cycles for fuel economy, power split, and SOC behavior.

  • UDDS: best mpg via low-speed electric assist + frequent regen.
  • HWFET: mid-range mpg; higher drag and fewer regen events.
  • US06: lowest mpg due to high power demand; regen spikes possible.

2) PMAC Motor Drive — Sine-Triangle PWM + 3rd Harmonic

DC source + LC filter → three-phase inverter → PMAC machine with dq-current control. Sine–triangle PWM with third-harmonic injection increases modulation headroom; torque steps (0 → ±400 Nm) at 500 rpm validate response.

  • dq current tracking; phase voltages/currents.
  • DC-link dynamics vs battery current under load/regen.

3) Battery Performance — Equivalent Circuit & Efficiency

First-order equivalent-circuit Li-ion model with OCV–SOC table and Coulomb counting. Compared round-trip efficiency under 0.2C vs 2C cycles and estimated energy for an aggressive drive window.

  • 0.2C/0.2C: highest efficiency (lowest losses).
  • 2C/0.2C: moderate efficiency; fast charge raises losses.
  • 2C/2C: lowest efficiency due to high current both ways.
